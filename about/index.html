About Us

We are a group of college students currently studying game design at the New Jersey Institute of Technology. We all naturally keep up with the events is the industry and quiet often we have discussions about recent events and share our opinions. These discussions tend to be both thought provoking and hilarious because quite often our opinions do not match. For example our debate about lives in modern video games was bloody…

State of the Industry aims to discuss developments in both game design/development and as the name implies the state of the industry as a whole. We will discuss everything from company’s business models and how that affects both their sales and image to mechanics in new games and how we feel they will be received both by critics and fans alike.

Since we’re just poor nobodies with no one to answer to, the opinions we present will be entirely our own (mostly) uncensored thoughts. We haven’t sold out yet… however if anyone wants us to sell out, just show us where we need to sign.
